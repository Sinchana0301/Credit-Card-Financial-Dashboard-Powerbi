### Credit-Card-Financial-Dashboard-Powerbi  
## Project Overview:  
This project is a Power BI Credit Card Financial Dashboard designed to analyze credit card transactions and customer behavior.
The dashboard provides insights into revenue, interest earned, customer income, transaction trends, and spending patterns.
It helps businesses understand customer segments and financial performance through interactive visualizations.
## Objectives:
- Analyze credit card revenue and transactions
- Track interest earned and financial performance
- Understand customer demographics
- Identify top customer segments
- Monitor quarterly and weekly revenue trends
- Visualize spending and transaction patterns
## Dashboards Included: 
1. Credit Card Transaction Report:
- Total Revenue
- Total Interest Earned
- Total Transaction Amount
- Total Transactions Count
- Revenue by Card Category
- Quarterly Revenue and Transaction Volume
- Revenue by Expenditure Type
- Revenue by Education
- Revenue by Customer Job
- Revenue by Use Chip
- Revenue by Acquisition Cost
2. Credit Card Customer Report:
- Revenue and Interest Overview
- Weekly Revenue Trend
- Revenue by Age Group
- Revenue by Gender
- Revenue by Customer Job
- Top 5 Sales by State
- Revenue by Marital Status
- Revenue by Income Group
- Revenue by Dependents
- Revenue by Education
## Tools & Technologies:  
- Power BI
- Excel
- SQL
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Visualization
- Business Intelligence
## Key Insights:  
- Blue card category generates highest revenue
- Swipe transactions contribute most revenue
- Graduate customers generate highest revenue
- Businessman and White-collar customers contribute more revenue
- High income group customers generate maximum revenue
- Quarterly revenue increases in Q4
